About

Jessica Ahn, born in 2003 in Chicago, Illinois, is an artist residing in Columbia, Missouri, completing a BA in Art at the School of Visual Studies at the University of Missouri. Before going to university, she resided in St. Louis, which is where she grew inspired to become an artist. Primarily working with painting and ceramics, she continues to expand her horizons through other mediums such as yarn and chalk.

While attending school, she has taken part in a community mural project, titled Stuff Your Eyes With Wonder, which is located in the stairwell of the Fine Arts Building at the University of Missouri. She has also taken a year to study abroad in Seoul, South Korea, exploring both her own cultural roots, as well as viewing art through a Korean lens. Her expected graduation is December 2025.
